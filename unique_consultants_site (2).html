About Us

Unique Whanganui Consultants provides trusted, kaupapa Māori facilitation and governance support for whānau, hapū, and iwi. Our approach is grounded in tikanga and mana motuhake, with a focus on reconnecting people with their whenua.

Services

  • Facilitation of Māori Land Court and Trust Hui
  • Charter Development and Governance Reviews
  • Strategic Planning and Organisational Advice
  • Whānau and Hapū Engagement Processes
  • Independent Mediation and Reporting
